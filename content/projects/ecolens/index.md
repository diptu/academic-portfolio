An AI-powered carbon accounting and sustainability platform, designed with the architectural
depth required for enterprise-grade, multi-tenant deployment.

- Multi-tenant system architecture supporting isolated organizational data and reporting
- Carbon intelligence: emission tracking and AI-assisted estimation from partial energy data
- ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) reporting workflows
- Enterprise AI systems: REST APIs with tenant-aware authentication and authorization

**Core stack:** FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Next.js, Azure, Docker, Redis, Celery
